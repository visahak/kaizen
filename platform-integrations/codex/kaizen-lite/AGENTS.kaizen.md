## Kaizen Codex Workflow

For substantive coding, debugging, implementation, and repository-investigation tasks in this repository:

- Invoke `$kaizen-workflow` before other substantive work.
- `$kaizen-workflow` must follow this exact sequence: `$kaizen-recall` -> task work -> `$kaizen-learn`.
- Do not skip or reorder the workflow.
- If recall returns no entities, proceed normally.
- If learn finds no new high-signal entities, that still counts as successful completion.
- Saved entities must stay high-signal: non-obvious, project-specific, and grounded in what actually happened during the task.
